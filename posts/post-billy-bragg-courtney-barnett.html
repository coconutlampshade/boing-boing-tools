Billy Bragg and Courtney Barnett performing "Sunday Morning" will warm your heart

Every few months, my algorithm serves up one utterly delightful video: Billy Bragg singing The Velvet Underground's "Sunday Morning" with Courtney Barnett. It appeared again just now on what is indeed, currently, for me, Sunday morning. I find their performance to be pure, joyful perfection.

The original "Sunday Morning" was released as a single by the Velvet Underground and Nico in 1966 and appears as the opening track on their 1967 debut. This version was recorded on May 5, 2014, at RocKwiz, an Australian music-themed TV trivia show featuring guest artists performing with the house band. It was just two years after Barnett's first EP and a year before her breakthrough album Sometimes I Sit and Think, and Sometimes I Just Sit.

Their voices blend beautifully, and I have to say, I prefer their version to the original. I adore how gleeful Courtney looks performing with such a legend â€” she keeps glancing at Billy with wide, happy eyes and a big smile. One commenter agreed: "Every 15 seconds or so, Courtney goes 'Ahhhh I'm performing with Billy Bragg,' and just looks so happy."

It's also heart-warming to see how supportive Billy is throughout. And Courtney's guitar solo is genuinely fire. They really are perfect together.
