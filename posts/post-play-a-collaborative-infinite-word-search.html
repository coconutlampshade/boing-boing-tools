Play a collaborative infinite word search

Finished the crossword, Wordle, and Clues by Sam? Skip Strands and try Words.zip, an infinite collaborative word search using a GitHub repository of over 450,000 words. The center section is mostly filled, so zoom out to find workable areas. The internet being the internet, folks are getting creative with "art" through strategic word placement.

No account required, but logging in with Google saves stats across devices.

Also check out monkeys.zip by the same creator. Monkeys.zip is an infinite monkey Shakespeare simulator. The monkeys recently finished typing every five-letter word from Shakespeare's works. Contributors earned Shakespeare hats as rewards. Log in and claim your monkeys to help reproduce Hamlet. Several "making of" posts explain the creation process, including the naming system for over fifteen thousand monkeys.
