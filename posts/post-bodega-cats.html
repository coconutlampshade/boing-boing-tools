Bodega Cats documents the hard-working felines of New York convenience stores

Alas, not even cats are immune to the crushing yoke of capitalism. What is the world coming to when even our wonderful creatures are forced to get jobs?

At least they look cute doing it. In my continued search for the strangest niches on the Internet (that won't put this website on a watchlist), the Bodega Cats Bluesky account is one of my favorites. As the name implies, it documents the hard-working cats found in convenience stores across New York and beyond. Look at this one guarding the ATM. What a good job he's doing.

This one's qualified to handle your payment, as long as it's in lunchmeat.

If you need a spot of brightness in your day — and don't we all — it doesn't get much better than a scroll through Bodega Cats.
